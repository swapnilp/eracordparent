A common platform for teachers, institution and administrative departments to work towards a common goal of helping the students to learn and score better which in turn improves their performance and help achieve a competitive output.
Works on four basic functions -
  • planning
  • organizing
  • controlling
  • motivating

One time Registration process that is simple and straight forward.

Click on Get Started and register with your Name and a valid Mobile Number. Once you register you will get a one type password on your registered mobile number to create a 4 Digit MPIN which you use to access your account.
